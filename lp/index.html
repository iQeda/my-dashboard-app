FEATURES

Everything you need

A minimal, keyboard-driven launcher for your daily tools.

App & URL Launcher
Launch Mac apps and browser sites with one click from a single dashboard.
Workspaces
Group related apps & sites into workspaces. Open all at once with a single action.
Keyboard First
⌘K command palette, arrow keys, Enter to launch. No mouse required.
Categories
Organize items by category with visual separators and drag-to-reorder.
iCloud Sync
Config auto-saved to iCloud. Seamless sync across all your Macs.
Dark Mode
Automatically follows macOS appearance. Light and dark, always native.
HOW IT WORKS

How it works

Three steps to a faster workflow.

01Dashboard

See everything at a glance. Favorites, pinned workspaces, categories.

02Browse

Browse all items grouped by category. Filter, sort, search.

03Launch

Press ⌘K to instantly search and launch any item.
